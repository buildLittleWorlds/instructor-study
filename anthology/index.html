The Anthology

A museum of experimental paper-apps — visit the exhibits, steal the patterns
Every specimen here is a piece of research or explanation that a reader can do something to. When you visit one, answer four questions: What does the reader get to do? How does the interaction strengthen the argument? What evidence or system sits underneath? What pattern could I borrow at smaller scale?

The collection grows all course long. New acquisitions get added weekly — and the best student paper-apps are accessioned into the Cohort Wing by class vote.
