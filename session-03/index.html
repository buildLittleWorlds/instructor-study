What made me curious

The word bank can mean a river's edge or a place that holds money. I wanted an interaction that lets a reader feel how surrounding words help settle which meaning fits.

What to notice

Transformer attention allows information at one token position to connect with information at other positions inside the permitted context. Those connections help create context-sensitive representations: the same word can behave differently in different sentences.

A counter-model, not an equivalence. A Conway's Game of Life cell checks eight immediate neighbors, and influence travels farther through repeated steps. A transformer token can connect to a distant position through attention. A transformer is not a cellular automaton, and a CA neighborhood is not attention.
